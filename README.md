# Pangaea

The evolution of data center architecture has now entered the phase of disaggregation, moving beyond the simple integration of computing resources toward the flexible composition and decomposition of these assets. In the current landscape, marked by the explosive growth of artificial intelligence and massive data processing workloads, overcoming the memory bottleneck is no longer a choice but a matter of survival.

The open-sourcing of Samsung’s Pangaea v2 orchestration software stands as a monumental milestone, extending innovative next-generation memory management technologies—once the exclusive domain of well-capitalized hyperscalers—across the entire industry ecosystem. Through this initiative, Enterprise data centers are empowered to transcend physical hardware limitations and realize true cloud-native Software-Defined Infrastructure.

We hope that the open innovation ecosystem catalyzed by Pangaea v2 will bridge the technological gap across the industry and become a powerful driving force accelerating the era of next-generation memory-centric computing.
